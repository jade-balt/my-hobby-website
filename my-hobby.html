My favorite hobby: listening to music

My favorite hobby is listening to music! I listen to music when I run, do my homework, and when I am getting ready to go somewhere! I always listen to music when I am writing essays because it helps me think. I also like to listen to music when I am getting ready to go somewhere because it makes the process less boring.

It is my favorite hobby because it makes anything I do more fun because there is music in the background. For example, when I am online shopping I always listenn to music. I also like to listen to music when I just hanging out with friends or even just by myself. I also like to listen to music when I am in the pool or tanning. Somtimes in the morning on the way to school I listen to music too.

I like listening to music so this summer I went to a music festival called Firefly. Firefly is a four day festival in Dover, Delawere. Four day passes cost 250$ on sale and 300$ full price. I ordered my tickets for sale!

I liked Firefly because I like to listen to music and when I went I saw many artists and bands there that I like listening to. Some of these bands/artists include Florence and the Machine, Fetty Wap, the 1975, Blink 182, Tame Impala, and A$AP ROCKY. I had a pretty good view for all of the concerts I went to and I really liked the lineup this year since many of the people performing I usually had listened to on a regular day.
